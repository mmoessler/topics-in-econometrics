8.2 Specification

\[\begin{align} R_{0t} = \alpha \tilde{\beta}' \begin{pmatrix} R_{1t}^{(0)} \\ \vdots \\ R_{1t}^{(\hat{m})} \end{pmatrix} + e_t, \end{align}\]

where

\[\begin{align} \tilde{\beta}' = (\beta_0', \dots, \beta_{\hat{m}}')' \end{align}\]

and

\[\begin{align} R_{1t}^{(j)} = R_{1t} \mathds{1}_{\{\hat{t}_j < t \leq \hat{t}_{j+1} \}} \;\;\; \text{for} \;\;\; 0 \leq j \leq \hat{m} \end{align}\]
